# Paper Guide -- State Transition Circuit Benchmarks (RU-V2)

## Classification

**Tier: WORKSHOP / POSTER**

This paper presents a systematic benchmark of ZK state transition circuits across
7 configurations (3 depths x 3 batch sizes). The exact constraint formula and scaling
analysis are useful contributions, but the circuit design (chained Merkle proof
verification) is standard in the field. Best suited for a workshop or poster session.
