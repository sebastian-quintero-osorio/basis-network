# Paper Guide -- Sparse Merkle Trees with Poseidon Hash (RU-V1)

## Classification

**Tier: INTERNAL TECHNICAL REPORT**

This paper documents the benchmarking and implementation of a depth-32 SMT with
Poseidon hash. The results (insert 1.825ms, proof gen 0.018ms, verify 1.744ms)
validate the design choice but do not advance the state of the art. The Poseidon
vs MiMC comparison is well-known in the field. Valuable as internal reference
documentation for the engineering team.
