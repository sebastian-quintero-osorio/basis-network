\subsection{Proof System Elimination}

Table~\ref{tab:elimination} summarizes the constraint-based
elimination of candidates.

\begin{table}[htbp]
\caption{Proof System Elimination Against Enterprise Constraints}
\label{tab:elimination}
\centering
\begin{tabular}{@{}lccccc@{}}
\toprule
\textbf{Constraint} & \textbf{G16} & \textbf{P-KZG} & \textbf{h2-KZG} & \textbf{h2-IPA} & \textbf{py2} \\
\midrule
C1: Gas $<$500K      & 220K  & 290K  & 420K  & Fail  & Fail \\
C2: Proof $<$1KB     & 128B  & 868B  & 900B  & Fail  & Fail \\
C3: Time $<$2$\times$& Ref   & Pass  & Pass  & Pass  & Pass \\
C4: Universal SRS    & Fail  & Pass  & Pass  & Pass  & Pass \\
C5: BN254 EVM        & Pass  & Pass  & Pass  & Fail  & Fail \\
C6: Custom gates     & Fail  & Pass  & Pass  & Pass  & Pass \\
C7: Recursion        & Fail  & Pass  & Pass  & Pass  & Pass \\
C8: Rust-native      & Pass  & Pass  & Pass  & Pass  & Pass \\
\midrule
\textbf{Verdict}     & Base  & Cand  & \textbf{Sel}   & Elim  & Elim \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\begin{flushleft}
\footnotesize G16 = Groth16, P-KZG = PLONK-KZG, h2-KZG = halo2-KZG,
h2-IPA = halo2-IPA, py2 = plonky2. Sel = Selected, Cand = Candidate,
Elim = Eliminated, Base = Baseline.
\end{flushleft}
\end{table}

\textbf{plonky2 eliminated}: Proof size of 43--130~KB fails C2 by
40--130$\times$. Native EVM verification costs $\sim$18M gas (fails
C1). The Goldilocks field is incompatible with BN254 precompiles
(fails C5). Polygon has deprecated plonky2 in favor of
plonky3~\cite{plonky2}.

\textbf{halo2-IPA eliminated}: Pallas/Vesta curves have no EVM
precompile support (fails C5). Proof size of 1--3~KB is marginal
on C2. Verification requires $O(\log n)$ EC operations on
non-standard curves, making on-chain verification impractical
(fails C1).

\textbf{halo2-KZG selected}: Passes all constraints. Production-proven
at Scroll, Axiom, and Taiko on Ethereum mainnet.
