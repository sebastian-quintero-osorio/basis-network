\subsection{Inner Proof Baseline}

Table~\ref{tab:inner-proof} presents the inner proof measurements across
30 iterations. Each enterprise produces a single halo2-KZG proof over
BN254 for an 8-step state transition circuit.

\begin{table}[htbp]
\centering
\caption{Inner Proof Measurements (30 Iterations)}
\label{tab:inner-proof}
\begin{tabular}{lrl}
\toprule
\textbf{Metric} & \textbf{Value} & \textbf{Notes} \\
\midrule
Proof size       & 640 bytes  & halo2-KZG (BN254) \\
Prove time (mean) & 19.7\,ms  & Per enterprise \\
Prove time StdDev & $< 2$\,ms & CV $< 10\%$ \\
Verify time       & $< 1$\,ms & Via halo2 \texttt{verify\_proof} \\
Verify time StdDev & $< 0.8$\,ms & Stable \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{table}

The 640-byte proof size is consistent with the 672-byte measurement
from RU-L9 (PLONK migration study). The 19.7\,ms prove time reflects
the smaller circuit (8 steps vs.\ 500 steps in RU-L9's extended
benchmark).
