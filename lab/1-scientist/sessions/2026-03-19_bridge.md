## Key Findings

- **Deposit latency**: ~4.6s (default), 60x faster than zkSync Era due to Avalanche sub-2s finality
- **Withdrawal latency**: ~21.1s (default), 130x faster than zkSync Era (no 3h security delay)
- **Zero-fee advantage**: All bridge operations are free on Basis L1, removing economic barriers for escape hatch
- **Keccak256 withdraw trie**: 48K gas for proof verification (vs ~160K for Poseidon)
- **Escape hatch**: 24h timeout, 118.5K gas total, viable even for small balances (zero-fee)
