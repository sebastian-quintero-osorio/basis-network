## Decisions Made

1. **Atomic Deposits**: Modeled L1 deposit + L2 credit as one atomic action. The relayer
   is trusted and enterprise-operated, making this abstraction safe for protocol-level
   verification.

2. **Merkle Proof Abstraction**: Cryptographic proof verification abstracted away. The
   spec verifies the state machine, not the crypto primitives.

3. **Sequencer Liveness Guard**: Added `~sequencerAlive` to `ActivateEscapeHatch` as a
   modeling simplification (contract does not check this). Documented as abstraction.

4. **MaxWithdrawals Bound**: Added to prevent unbounded withdrawal ID growth from
   deposit/withdraw cycling. Without this, TLC state space was intractable (100M+ states
   at depth 20 with queue still growing).

5. **BalanceConservation Formulation**: Used exact equality pre-escape, inequality (>=)
   during escape. The gap during escape is the known escape hatch limitation (post-
   finalization deposits not capturable by escape mechanism).
